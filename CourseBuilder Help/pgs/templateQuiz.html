Using the Quiz Template

The Quiz template is used to create simple True/False or Multiple Choice quizzes.

Using the Quiz Template:

  1. Open your Actions panel and click frame 1 of the actions layer in the timeline.
  2. In the Actions panel, enter your question, answers, correct answer and feedback.
  3. Compile the swf using ⌘-return and close your fla.
