Using the Welcome Template

The welcome template is used to introduce your course. It is a simple splash introduction.

Using the Welcome Template:

  1. Click on the only movie clip on the stage (instance of replace).
  2. From the Properties panel, click Swap...
  3. Choose a movie clip from the Full Page templates folder in the dialog box.
  4. Enter your Course Code, Course Title, and Course Description.
  5. Or... Create your own Welcome Page.
  6. Compile your swf using ⌘-return and close your fla.
